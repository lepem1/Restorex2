How can I integrate RestoreX with my server?

Simply click the "Invite" button, authenticate with your Discord account, and select the server you wish to secure. Ensure the bot has administrative permissions for anti-nuke to work properly.

Does the Anti-Nuke system require configuration?

Yes, while RestoreX comes with smart defaults, you can deeply customize thresholds and trusted roles through our intuitive setup commands to perfectly match your server's needs.

Can I get analytics for my Discord server activities?

Our logging & alerts system provides real-time tracking of infractions, destructive actions, and moderation history so you're always informed.
